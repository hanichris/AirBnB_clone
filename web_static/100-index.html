Wakanda

$65
4 Guests
2 Bedrooms
1 Bathroom
Owner: John Legend

Finally a nice and clean, well maintained lake home close to Sioux Falls at a reasonable price! Take a 30 minute drive to Lake Vermillion and see this A frame lake home built in 1984 with a full tuck under garage. The view of Lake Vermillion is Soothing and Peaceful and where memories are made. What a way to spend the weekends, vacations or year around. This home has had updates including siding, windows and recently HVAC and shingles. Possibly build another structure since the property includes lots 13, 14 and 15. The house sits on lot 15. Serviced by septic, propane and TM Rural Water with a cistern as bonus. Lake Vermillion is a 512-acre reservoir is located 27 miles west of Sioux Falls and is easily accessible off I-90. It has become a popular lake among fishermen, boaters and swimmers. The lake has a strong population of walleye, northern pike, crappie and blue gill. There are two boat ramps on the lake and a fishing pier. Owner is a licensed real estate agent in SD.
