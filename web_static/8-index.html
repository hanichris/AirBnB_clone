Finally a nice and clean, well maintained lake home close to Sioux Falls at a reasonable price! Take a 30 minute drive to Lake Vermillion and see this A frame lake home built in 1984 with a full tuck under garage. The view of Lake Vermillion is Soothing and Peaceful and where memories are made. What a way to spend the weekends, vacations or year around. This home has had updates including siding, windows and recently HVAC and shingles. Lake Vermillion is a 512-acre reservoir is located 27 miles west of Sioux Falls and is easily accessible off I-90. It has become a popular lake among fishermen, boaters and swimmers. The lake has a strong population of walleye, northern pike, crappie and blue gill. There are two boat ramps on the lake and a fishing pier. Owner is a licensed real estate agent in SD.
